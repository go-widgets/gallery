go-widgets gallery

Live wasm demo of go-widgets/toolkit.

Dashboard — every widget kind (Button, Toggle, CheckButton, RadioButton, Entry, SpinButton, Scale, DropDown, ProgressBar, LevelBar, Spinner, TextView, Calendar, ColorChooser, ListBox, TreeView, Notebook, Paned, Expander + Frame, Menu, Toolbar, Statusbar, Notification) side by side, mirroring GTK4's widget-factory layout at a glance. Click a menu name to open its popover; click any widget for a toast.
